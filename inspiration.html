Wooden materials


Historically, Wood has been the key material of choice for making furniture. This type of furniture is never out of style. Prospects for
wooden furniture altered with the industrial revolution.


Wooden furniture has increased in popularity over timr, despite a fall in output. There is somthing classic about wooden furniture. Wood is
perfect for nearly all styles and color combinations in interior design and has still remained relevant.



Durability

Wood lasts years with relatively little
upkeep

True hardwoods (cheery, white oak,
walnut) never lose good quality.

Wooden furniture constructed of
hardwoods may survive for decades

Easy to maintain

Cleaning wood only requires routine
dusing.

A moist microfiber may be used to
wipe up spills and sticky messes.

You wont have to be concerned about
rus in the future.

Eco-friendly

Wood is a resource capable of
being susained, unlike plastic.

We can reduce carbon emissions
by buying sustainable timber more

It's crucial to purchase wood from
sustainable fores
